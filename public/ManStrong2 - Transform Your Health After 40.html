About The Founder - Dr. Suren Naidoo

Private GP • Aesthetic Doctor • Men's Health Specialist

Manstrong 2 program is built by Dr. Suren Naidoo. He is a private GP and one of the most experienced and successful aesthetic doctors in London. He has more than 35 years of experience in managing medical emergencies as well as catering to routine requirements. He has a special interest in cardiovascular medicine and type 2 Diabetes.

Dr Naidoo has a keen interest in men's health, primarily focussing on men aged 40 to 70 with Type 2 Diabetes. Empowering them to reverse their diabetes, to improve erectile dysfunction and prevent silent heart attacks.

Dr Naidoo is a trained graduate in medicine from the Royal College of Surgeons, Ireland in 1978 and underwent his postgraduate training at the Royal Liverpool Hospital and Ormskirk District General Hospital. He maintains a keen interest in medical education and has been involved with teaching and examining students at University College London. He is also a GP appraiser.
