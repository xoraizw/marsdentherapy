Mental and Health Awareness

Your trusted mental health resource directory

Since 2004, Mental and Health Awareness has served as a trusted online directory and educational resource for individuals, families, professionals, and advocates seeking reliable information related to mental health, educational needs, emotional well-being, and behavioral health.
We connect users with curated directories, educational articles, events, and support tools designed to help people better understand available options and make informed decisions.
Our resources place a special focus on adolescents and young adults, while remaining inclusive of individuals and families at every stage of life.
Mental and Health Awareness does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Our mission is to offer clear, accessible information and connections to trusted resources that support mental wellness and informed decision-making.
