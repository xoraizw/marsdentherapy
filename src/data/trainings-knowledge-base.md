## 1. Leadership Training

**Summary:** Raises leaders' confidence and authority; helps them define purpose and vision and execute it. Covers transforming resistance into agreement, ownership thinking, giving instructions successfully, mastering emotions, overcoming obstacles, implementing change. Uses Time Matrix strategic planning. For supervisors, team leaders, managers, directors, executives, women entrepreneurs. Formats: keynotes, individual/team coaching, half-day to five-day programs. Hands-on with exercises; participants leave with reflective follow-up assignments.

**Context:** How much is ineffective and inconsistent leadership costing you? Leaders learn to exude authority while honing relationship quality; leadership thinking, stance and presence are boosted. Participants are tested in their leadership styles and develop their unique way of achieving results in real time.

## 2. Presentation Excellence

**Summary:** Boosts presentation presence and impact; overcomes nervousness; improves body, voice, and non-verbal messaging. Topics: making content simple and clear, opening presentations, handling Q&A, body/movement/gesture/voice, charisma, engagement and buy-in. For executives, managers, sales, anyone speaking to groups. Video-taped presentations and individualized step-by-step feedback. Formats: keynotes, coaching, half-day to five-day programs.

**Context:** Your presentation reflects you and your company. Modules can be custom-designed; includes overcoming nervousness, building rapport with the audience, well-prepared commencements, and positive first impressions.

## 3. Emotional Intelligence

**Summary:** 85% of personal and professional success is linked to how we handle ourselves and engage others. Covers letting go of worry/overthinking, shifting stress into productive attitudes, overcoming anger/frustration, transforming sadness/depression, resolving conflict, emotionally engaging with empathy. Builds Self Awareness, Self Management, Social Awareness, Relationship Management. Pre- and post-assessments; hands-on, interactive. Formats: keynotes, coaching, half-day to five-day programs.

**Context:** EQ is the only intelligence you can improve by working on yourself. Participants meet and transform emotions at the onset; professional relationships based on EI are more effective and successful.

## 4. Accountability & Efficiency Booster

**Summary:** Addresses fragmented team dynamics, wasted time, and poor meeting preparation. Empowers ownership and accountability; reduces time lost to ineffective communication and conflict. Participants report recovering significant hours per day. Formats: keynotes, individual/team coaching, half-day to five-day programs. Follow-up procedures for long-term success.

**Context:** Do fragmented and unproductive team dynamics increase stress and hamper workflow? Program focuses on ownership thinking and productive attitudes; sample results include managers recovering more than two hours per day.

## 5. Business Communication

**Summary:** Reduces conflict and miscommunication; everyone takes responsibility for communication. Maintains rapport across differences (culture, age, gender). Teaches passing on challenging messages while keeping relationships. Improves instructions being followed and team responsibility. Hands-on; includes leading critical conversations and confrontation. Formats: keynotes, coaching, half-day to five-day programs.

**Context:** Do you know how much conflict and miscommunication is costing your organization? Participants practice passing on challenging messages while maintaining relationships; benefits include improvements in leading critical conversations and confrontation.

## 6. Customer Service

**Summary:** Builds excellent service attitude and responsiveness; reduces customer complaints. Covers ethics, courtesy, friendliness under stress; addressing emotionally charged situations and complaints; creating business relationships and accountability. Employees connect with anyone in any situation; includes follow-up sessions, email drills, video conferences, yearly review. Formats: keynotes, coaching, half-day to five-day programs.

**Context:** Are you losing customers to competitors because of missing customer service? Program includes the art of emotionally engaging with customers so they want to come back and refer others. Same skills apply internally within teams.
